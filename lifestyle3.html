BLACKPINK’s Jisoo And Lisa Keep Promise And Delight With Adorable Airport Fashion

BLACKPINK’s Lisa and Jisoo had a special surprise prepared as they kept a promise that was previously made!

On December 3, BLACKPINK appeared at Gimpo International Airport to head to Japan for their “In Your Area” world tour.

What surprised everyone were the outfits Jisoo and Lisa were wearing. Jisoo had on a pink rabbit onesie and a headband with rabbit ears on it, paired with a turtle shell on her back. Meanwhile, Lisa wore a duck onesie and matched them with duck-themed socks and shoes as well.

It was revealed that Jisoo and Lisa were actually keeping a promise made during their “2019 Private Stage [Chapter 1]” fan meeting in September. Jennie and Rosé faced off against Jisoo and Lisa in a series of games during the fan meeting, and as they lost both games, Jisoo and Lisa had promised to prepare a special airport look in the future.

Jisoo also shared photos of them posing in their outfits on her personal Instagram account.
